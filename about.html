MUSICAL JOURNEY

Early Discovery

I discovered the beauty of music at an early age. I remember the first time I enjoyed singing was in primary school. Our teacher taught us a song for children - it was a canon, and I adored the fact we could all sing in choir, creating harmony together.

First Guitar

When I was 14, I learned my first three chords on a guitar. From that day, I knew music would be my lifelong companion. It gave me calm and became my escape from life's challenges. With hindsight, I know every struggle was necessary for my growth.

"Without tension on the strings, we wouldn't be able to hear their sound. With pain comes growth and beauty."
First Recording

I discovered the mystery of recording when my mother gifted me a tape recorder. Hearing my voice for the first time was a strange experience, but I grew to love it. This became my training ground - I could hear the wrong notes and try again and again until I got it right. It was marvelous.

Today

Music continues to be my language of choice when words fall short. Every song tells a story, every melody carries emotion, and every performance is a chance to connect souls through the universal language of music.
