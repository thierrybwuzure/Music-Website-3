Fear and Hatred

Cloaked evil comes as light
Urging to wear a mask of fear
A veil of anxiety and submission
Breeding hatred in the heart

But love conquers all
Breaking chains of darkness
Setting the captive free
With wings of hope and peace

No weapon formed against love
Shall ever prosper
For light overcomes darkness
And truth destroys all lies

Stand firm in the light
Let courage be your shield
And love your mighty sword
Against fear and hatred's yield

Seapiece

I wish rest never cease
That I could sail overseas
Through the ocean of stillness
Where waves whisper peace

Beyond the horizon's call
Where seabirds dance and soar
In symphony with the wind
Upon the endless shore

The salt air fills my lungs
With dreams of distant lands
Where time moves like the tide
Gentle as shifting sands

In this moment of grace
I find my soul's release
Floating on liquid light
In perfect, endless peace
